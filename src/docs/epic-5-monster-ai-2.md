# Epic 5: Monster AI 2.0 - Personality & Factions

This epic makes the dungeon inhabitants feel more alive and reactive.

## User Stories

### Personality Traits
- **As a player,** I want some monsters to flee when low on health (Cowardice) and others to wait for reinforcements before attacking (Tactical).

### Ranged Enemies
- **As a player,** I want to encounter archers and mages who try to stay at a distance while attacking me.

### Factions
- **As a player,** I want to see different types of monsters fighting each other (e.g., Orcs vs. Spiders), allowing me to use their enmities to my advantage.
