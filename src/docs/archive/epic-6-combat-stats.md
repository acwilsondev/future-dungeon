# Epic 6: Combat & Stats System

This epic details the mechanics of conflict and character progression.

## User Stories

### Tactical Combat
- **As a player,** I want to engage in turn-based combat with monsters by moving into them, so that the gameplay remains non-modal and focused on the grid.
- **As a player,** I want combat to be determined by stats like Health, Strength, and Defense, so that my character's growth feels meaningful.

### Feedback
- **As a player,** I want to see clear feedback when I hit or miss a monster (and vice versa), so that I can understand the outcome of my actions.
- **As a player,** I want to see my current health and other vital stats at all times, so that I can make informed survival decisions (Numbers).

### Progression
- **As a player,** I want to gain experience points for defeating monsters and level up, increasing my stats and survivability.
