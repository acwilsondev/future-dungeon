# Epic 4: Status Effects & Temporal Mechanics

This epic introduces time-based effects and deeper tactical considerations.

## User Stories

### Duration-based Effects
- **As a player,** I want to be able to poison enemies or be poisoned myself, with health ticking down over several turns.
- **As a player,** I want to use potions of Speed or Strength that provide temporary buffs.

### Turn Ticking
- **As a developer,** I want a centralized "Turn System" that handles the expiration of status effects and environmental ticks (like fire spreading).

### Visual Indicators
- **As a player,** I want to see icons or text in the sidebar indicating my current status effects and their remaining duration.
