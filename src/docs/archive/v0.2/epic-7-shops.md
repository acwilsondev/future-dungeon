# Epic 7: Economy - Wealth & Shops

This epic introduces a commerce system to manage surplus loot.

## User Stories

### Gold & Loot
- **As a player,** I want to find gold piles in the dungeon and earn gold by selling items I don't need.

### Merchants
- **As a player,** I want to occasionally find neutral Merchant NPCs who stay in a fixed location and offer a selection of items for sale.

### Trading UI
- **As a player,** I want a clear interface for buying and selling items, showing prices and my current gold balance.
