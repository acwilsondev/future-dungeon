# Epic 8: Item & Inventory System

This epic covers the management of resources and equipment.

## User Stories

### Resource Management
- **As a player,** I want to find and pick up items like health potions and food, so that I can manage my survival over long descents (Resource management).
- **As a player,** I want to have a limited inventory capacity, forcing me to make difficult choices about what to carry.

### Equipment
- **As a player,** I want to find and equip weapons and armor that improve my combat stats, so that I can tackle stronger monsters.
- **As a player,** I want to be able to drop items I no longer need to make room for better loot.

### Identification
- **As a player,** I want to encounter unidentified items whose effects I must discover through use or scrolls, adding a layer of risk and reward (Exploration and discovery).
