# Epic 5: Boss Encounters & Guardian AI

This epic introduces high-stakes challenges and memorable adversaries.

## User Stories

### Unique Guardians
- **As a player,** I want to encounter unique, named bosses (e.g., "The Broodmother," "The Skeleton King") at specific intervals in the dungeon.
- **As a player,** I want bosses to have larger or unique glyphs and distinct color palettes to distinguish them from standard monsters.

### Multi-Phase Combat
- **As a player,** I want bosses to change their behavior or unlock new abilities as their health decreases (e.g., summoning minions or changing attack patterns).

### Milestone Rewards
- **As a player,** I want bosses to drop powerful, guaranteed loot that helps me survive the deeper levels.

## Developer Goals
- Implement a "Scripted AI" system that can trigger events or behavior changes based on HP thresholds.
- Create custom room generation templates for "Boss Arenas."
