# Canon

- Aetheric entities created the material plane, focused on the Voidstar.
- Cult of the Star emerges
- Loss of the Voidstar
  - Due to unknown events, the Voidstar was lost to time.
- The Mana Wars
  - Without the centralizing force of the Voidstar, fracture into Solari/Nihil
- The Iron Ascendancy
  - Without a stablizing religious force, the materialist Iron Concord faction arose.
- Biomass Awakening
  - Fueled by energy from the Voidstar, the biomass at the bottom of the Future
    Dungeon began to evolve rapidly. It awakened as a hivemind shortly
    thereafter.
- The Biotic Eye
  - The final evolution of the biomass began as it transcended the material
  plane. It developed a spiritual power from the Voidstar and now exhibits
  traits of both Aetheric and Biomass power.
