### The Rat Monks

The **Rat Monks** are a peaceful ratfolk population who are guaranteed to spawn in a hidden corner of the dungeon in the early-mid (levels 20-40) game. Their lair is a **Rat Monastery** a single side-level with one entrance and exit to/from the main dungeon.

They are initially aligned with the player, as long as the player does not attack them.

The Rat Monks ask the player to bring back a unique, valuable item. When the player does, the Rat Monks grant a martial arts boon (improving their unarmed attacks).
