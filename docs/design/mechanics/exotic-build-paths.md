# Exotic Character Build Paths

In the *Future Dungeon* system, character progression is defined by how the player interacts with the **[Voidstar](../world/lore.md)**—the octahedral source of power at the center of the celestial conflict. While the **[Fighter](player.md)** provides a reliable physical foundation of **Iron**-tier equipment and high attributes, these paths represent radical shifts in how a player engages with the ASCII grid and the factions within it.

---

## **1. The Aligned Specialist (Solari or Nihil)**

By dedicating the full **[5-pip mana limit](magic.md)** to one side of the "As Above, So Below" spectrum, the player becomes a master of metaphysical state modification.

* **Solari (Orange):** A "Nova Paragon" who focuses on **Additive States**. Gameplay centers on **Shielding**, **Damage Multipliers**, and **Radiance** to reveal and burn enemies.
* **Nihil (Purple):** A "Void Sovereign" who focuses on **Subtractive States**. Gameplay centers on **Life-Leech**, **Entropy** (armor degradation), and **Gravity** to control enemy positioning.
* **Systemic Interaction:** High-tier specialization unlocks Level 5 spells but draws the maximum ire of the **Biotic Eye** boss.

## **2. Aetheric Equilibrium (The "Secret" Path)**

This path utilizes the "Glitch" logic of the **Aetheric** creator-race by maintaining a delicate balance between the two celestial forces.

* **Requirement:** Maintain exactly **2 Solari and 2 Nihil mana pips**.
* **Systemic Hook:** Unlocks **Cyan (Aetheric)** "Grid Violation" tools.
* **Key Verbs:**
  * **Phase:** Move through walls (`walkable: false` tiles).
  * **Blink:** Instant coordinate changes that bypass traps.
  * **Flicker:** A passive evasion chance by "desyncing" from the turn.

## **3. The Primal Hive (The "Becoming")**

By rejecting all mana, the player remains "spiritually silent," allowing them to commune with and eventually become part of the **Biomass** ecosystem.

* **Requirement:** Maintain **0/0 Mana** and refuse all celestial meditation.
* **Systemic Hook:** Permanent **Mutations** replace standard **Iron** equipment slots (e.g., a **Chitinous Scythe** replacing the Main Hand weapon).
* **Faction Relation:** **Emerald** (Biomass) entities treat the player as neutral.
* **Advantage:** Complete immunity to **Mire** (movement cost) penalties and scaling off **CON** and **STR** rather than item stats.

## **4. The Iron Contra (The Industrialist)**

The path of "Pure Grit." This build rejects both magical and biological evolution in favor of maximizing mundane industrial technology and physical reliability.

* **Requirement:** Stays grounded in **Iron**-tier logic, ignoring all Mana and Mutations.
* **Systemic Hook:** Focuses on **Hard Collisions** and tactical tools.
* **Key Verbs:**
  * **Impact:** Massive knockback that triggers `Wall_Collision` damage.
  * **Barricade:** Manipulating the pathfinding engine by building walls in real-time.
  * **Obscure:** Blocking Line of Sight (`LOS`) using smoke.
* **Reliability:** Naturally **Hardened** against Energy Drains, EMPs, and metaphysical **Entropy**.

## Endings

**Solari**: With the religious mandate of the [Voidstar](../world/lore.md), the Solari launch a jihad on the nonbelievers and build a galactic theocracy.
**Nihil**: With the religious mandate of the [Voidstar](../world/lore.md), the Nihil accelerate the end of the universe, setting in motion a 1000yr plan to darken all stars.
**Aetheric:** By finding a perfect alignment and recovering the [Voidstar](../world/lore.md), the player ascends to the Aetheric plane. The Aetheric rebuild the galaxy in a more harmonious image.
**Iron Contra**: The player uses the [Voidstar](../world/lore.md) as an infinite source of power, entering a new era of galactic capitalism with a strict monopoly on energy.
**Biomass:** By acquiring the [Voidstar](../world/lore.md) and unifying with the Biomass, the Player becomes the Hivemind. The Biomass takes over the galaxy.
