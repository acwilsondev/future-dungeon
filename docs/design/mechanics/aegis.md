# Aegis

*Aegis* is a technology or spell effect that grants fast-recharging hitpoints above your natural hitpoints. It functions as a damage soak.

Aegis recharges much faster than Health heals. When your Aegis is damaged, you gain AegisDrought(5) (see [status_effects.md](status_effects.md)). When your Aegis is depleted, you gain AegisDrought(10).

Otherwise, Aegis recovers 1 point per turn.
