# Epic 6: Experience & Progression

This epic gives the player a long-term sense of growth and customization.

## User Stories

### XP Gain
- **As a player,** I want to earn experience points for every monster I defeat, with more difficult monsters granting more XP.

### Leveling Up
- **As a player,** I want to see a "Level Up!" screen when I reach an XP threshold, allowing me to choose which stats (HP, ATK, DEF) to improve.

### Perks & Skills
- **As a player,** I want to occasionally unlock unique perks (e.g., "Toughness" for extra HP, "Eagle Eye" for more FOV) to define my character build.
