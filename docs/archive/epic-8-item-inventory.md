# Epic 7: Monster AI

This epic focuses on the behaviors of the inhabitants of the dungeon.

## User Stories

### Basic AI
- **As a player,** I want monsters to notice me when I am in their line of sight and start chasing me, so that they feel like active threats.
- **As a player,** I want monsters to attack me when they are adjacent, forcing me to use tactical positioning.

### Varied Behaviors
- **As a player,** I want different types of monsters to have different behaviors (e.g., some flee when low on health, others stay at range), providing a tactical challenge.
- **As a developer,** I want a simple way to define and assign AI behaviors to different monster types.

### Fairness
- **As a player,** I want monsters to follow the same basic rules of movement and combat that I do, ensuring the game feels fair (Monsters are similar to players).
