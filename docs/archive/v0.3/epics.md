# Epics Overview: RustLike v0.3 - "Echoes of the Ancients"

This initiative transforms the dungeon from a static gauntlet into a living, reactive environment with a clear ultimate goal.

## 1. Dynamic Lighting & Vision
Introduce light sources (torches, lamps, glowing crystals) that modify the field of view. Darkness now hides monsters more effectively and requires tactical use of light.

## 2. Environmental Hazard System
Implement "Chemistry" interactions: fire spreads on flammable surfaces, gas clouds expand and dissipate, and water can extinguish flames or conduct electricity.

## 3. Advanced Equipment & Paper Doll
Overhaul the item system to include specific equipment slots (Head, Torso, Hands, Feet, Finger). Add "Artifact" items with unique, non-stat properties.

## 4. Stealth & Sound Mechanics
Add a "Noise" component to actions. Moving fast or fighting creates sound that can alert nearby monsters, allowing for tactical stealth or distraction-based play.

## 5. Boss Encounters & Guardian AI
Design unique, named bosses with custom glyphs and multi-phase AI behaviors. These guardians will protect key milestones in the dungeon.

## 6. The Great Quest (Victory Condition)
Implement the primary game goal: retrieve the "Amulet of the Ancients" from the bottom floor and escape. Add victory and defeat states.

## 7. Identification & Alchemy
Introduce unidentified items (e.g., "Milky Potion"). Players must use or test items to identify them. Add an alchemy station to combine surplus items.

## 8. Thematic Dungeon Branches
Create sub-areas with unique generation rules, such as "The Overgrown Gardens" (heavy flora/poison) or "The Sunken Halls" (flooded tiles/undead).
